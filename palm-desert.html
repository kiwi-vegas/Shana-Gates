The Valley's Cultural Hub

Shopping, Arts &
Desert Elegance

Palm Desert sits at the heart of the Coachella Valley and has long been the preferred address for those who want it all — sophisticated shopping on the famous El Paseo "Rodeo Drive of the Desert," a thriving arts community, premier dining, and easy access to world-class golf.

From guard-gated luxury communities like The Reserve, Bighorn, and Desert Falls to charming single-family neighborhoods and active-adult enclaves, Palm Desert offers an extraordinary range of housing to match every lifestyle. It's also home to College of the Desert and the McCallum Theatre, making it one of the valley's most complete cities.

As Shana's home base and office location, she knows every street and cul-de-sac in Palm Desert intimately. Whether you're looking for a primary residence, vacation retreat, or investment property, she'll match you with the perfect home.

What Makes It Special

Living in
Palm Desert

🛍️

El Paseo Shopping

The "Rodeo Drive of the Desert" stretches a mile of luxury boutiques, art galleries, sidewalk cafés, and fine dining — all framed by mountain views and palm trees.

🎭

McCallum Theatre

The Coachella Valley's premier performing arts venue hosts Broadway productions, world-class musicians, and the acclaimed Off the Wall series — a true cultural anchor.

🎓

College of the Desert

A vibrant community college campus hosting farmers markets, enrichment programs, and contributing to Palm Desert's energetic, educated community identity.

⛳

Premier Golf

Desert Falls Country Club, Bighorn Golf Club, and The Reserve rank among the valley's most coveted fairways — all within Palm Desert's limits.

🏡

Diverse Neighborhoods

From ultra-luxury guard-gated estates to walkable family communities, Palm Desert offers the valley's broadest range of housing styles and price points.

🍷

Restaurant Row

El Paseo and surrounding streets host dozens of acclaimed restaurants — from casual brunch spots to white-tablecloth dining rooms with mountain panoramas.
