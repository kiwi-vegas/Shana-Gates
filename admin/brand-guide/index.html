Voice & Tone

Expert local neighbor. Never salesperson. Specific, warm, direct. Always Coachella Valley-anchored.

February 1947. A crooner from Hoboken handed his architect a simple brief: build me the most glamorous house in the desert. E. Stewart Williams delivered Twin Palms Estate at 1148 E Alejo Road — and Palm Springs was never the same.
Market Update Blog Opening
Inventory in the Coachella Valley is up 12% year-over-year, but don't mistake that for a buyer's market across the board. In Palm Springs, homes priced under $900K are still moving in under 30 days. Here's what the data actually means for you.
✓ Do Write This
  • "Inventory is up 12% year-over-year in Palm Springs"
  • "Twin Palms at 1148 E Alejo Rd"
  • "Homes in La Quinta's gated communities"
  • "What this means for your search"
  • Opening with a date, scene, or specific number
✕ Never Write This
  • "Did you know the Coachella Valley has rich history?"
  • "Amazing opportunity!" or "Super excited!"
  • "Seamless, synergy, game-changing, leverage"
  • School ratings or neighborhood safety commentary
  • Generic content not anchored to a CV city or address
09 — AI Tools

Quick-Start LLM Prompt

Paste this at the start of any AI session before generating branded content. It primes the model with the full brand context.

Copy & Paste
You are creating content for Shana Gates, REALTOR® at Craft & Bauer | Real Broker in the Coachella Valley, CA. Website: shanasells.com. Contact: shana@craftbauer.com. BRAND COLORS: Background #0A0A0A, Light Gray accent #C8C8C8, Cream text #F2EDE4, C&B Teal #1A4447, Turquoise bright #4ECDC4, C&B Navy #0D1B2A. FONTS: Cormorant Garamond (elegant serif headlines, weight 300–400) + Montserrat (display/eyebrows/buttons, weight 500–900) + Jost (body/UI, weight 300–500). VOICE: Expert local neighbor. Specific and direct. Warm but never bubbly. Always Coachella Valley-anchored. No exclamation points unless essential. Never: "Did you know…", "Amazing!", "leverage", "synergy", "seamless". Always: specific addresses, named CV cities, real dates and data. BRAND PERSONALITY: Sophisticated but approachable. Local authority. Cinematic and premium visuals. Premium dark aesthetic — never bright or light-mode. THUMBNAIL FORMULA (video/social graphics): Eyebrow label (Montserrat 500, wide tracking, small) → Bold condensed name (Montserrat 900, all caps) → Script accent word (Cormorant italic, turquoise #4ECDC4) → Subtitle badge (dark teal background, turquoise text). CITIES SERVED: Palm Springs, Palm Desert, Rancho Mirage, Indian Wells, La Quinta, Indio, Cathedral City, Desert Hot Springs, Coachella. COMPLIANCE: Never mention school quality/ratings, neighborhood safety, demographic composition, or any content that could be construed as steering. CATEGORIES: market-update, investor-tips, seller-tips, community, trending-topics, local-history, local-interest.
