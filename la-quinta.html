Golf, Resort & Growth

The Valley's
Rising Star

La Quinta has quietly become one of the most coveted addresses in the Coachella Valley — and for good reason. Anchored by the legendary La Quinta Resort & Spa and PGA West, the city offers a resort lifestyle that few communities anywhere in California can match.

With charming Old Town La Quinta at its heart — boutique shops, acclaimed restaurants, and a beloved farmers market — and the dramatic Santa Rosa Mountains as its backdrop, La Quinta strikes a perfect balance between natural beauty and modern luxury. The city's master-planned communities like Griffin Ranch, Andalusia, and Hideaway offer everything from golf villas to custom estate homes.

La Quinta's real estate market has been the valley's fastest appreciating over the past decade, driven by new development, resort amenities, and Coachella-era energy that has redefined the eastern valley as a premier destination.

What Makes It Special

Living in
La Quinta

⛳

PGA West

Five world-class golf courses — including the legendary Stadium Course — make PGA West one of golf's most iconic addresses. The WGC-American Express Championship is played here.

🏨

La Quinta Resort & Spa

One of California's most beloved resorts has anchored this city since 1926. Its hacienda-style casitas, pools, and spa have attracted Hollywood legends and modern travelers alike.

🏘️

Master-Planned Communities

Griffin Ranch, Hideaway, Andalusia, and Trilogy offer meticulously planned living with resort amenities, guard gates, and home designs that blend Spanish Colonial with desert modernism.

🛍️

Old Town La Quinta

A walkable village with boutique shops, acclaimed restaurants like Lavender Bistro, and a beloved Saturday farmers market framed by mountain views and bougainvillea.

⛰️

Cove Hiking & Trails

The Bear Creek Trail and La Quinta Cove offer spectacular hiking into the Santa Rosa foothills — steps from some of the valley's most beautiful residential neighborhoods.

📈

Fastest-Growing Market

La Quinta has led Coachella Valley appreciation rates for a decade. New luxury development continues to attract buyers who recognize the city's long-term value proposition.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions
About La Quinta

The questions buyers ask most when exploring La Quinta real estate.

What is the median home price in La Quinta?
+
La Quinta's median home price is approximately $600,000–$700,000 as of 2026, though the range is enormous. Affordable condos and townhomes near Highway 111 start around $350K, standard single-family homes in non-gated communities range from $450K–$800K, PGA West communities run $700K–$2M, and ultra-luxury estates at The Hideaway or Thermal Club can exceed $10M. La Quinta has something for almost every budget.
What is PGA West and why does it matter?
+
PGA West is one of the most famous golf resort communities in the world — home to six championship courses including the Stadium Course (site of the American Express PGA Tour event), the Jack Nicklaus Private Course, and the Greg Norman Course. Properties within PGA West communities carry a premium, attract buyers from across the country, and generate strong rental demand from golfers. The development spans over 2,000 acres and includes multiple guard-gated residential communities with their own HOA structures.
Is La Quinta good for new construction?
+
La Quinta is the most active new construction market in the Coachella Valley. Multiple builders are active across price points — from affordable KB Home communities to ultra-luxury Toll Brothers estates at Signature. If you're looking for a newly built desert home, La Quinta offers more options, better availability, and more competitive builder incentives than any other CV city. Shana works with all major builders and can negotiate on your behalf.
What is the Cove area of La Quinta?
+
La Quinta Cove is one of the most sought-after neighborhoods in the valley — a grid of older custom homes nestled directly against the Santa Rosa Mountains on the west side of Washington Street. Homes in the Cove offer unique privacy, direct trail access, mountain views, and a walkable, neighborhood feel uncommon in the CV. Price points range from $400K to $1.5M+ depending on size and condition. It's a favorite among buyers who want character over resort uniformity.
How is La Quinta for short-term rentals?
+
La Quinta allows short-term rentals and has a well-established STR permit system. Demand is strong year-round — PGA events, Coachella Festival proximity, and the valley's general tourism all drive bookings. Specific communities (especially guard-gated enclaves) may restrict or prohibit STRs via HOA rules — always verify before purchasing for rental income. Shana can identify STR-compliant properties and help you evaluate investment potential.
What's happening at SilverRock?
+
SilverRock Resort is La Quinta's signature development project — a mixed-use destination anchored by the SilverRock Championship Golf Course (host of the American Express PGA Tour event). The project includes a planned Auberge resort hotel, conference center, luxury resort residential lots, and a Village Park component. As development progresses, it is expected to significantly enhance property values in the surrounding area and position La Quinta as an even stronger destination resort market.
