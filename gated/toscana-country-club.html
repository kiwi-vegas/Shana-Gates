        Understanding HOA Fees

        HOA fees in gated Coachella Valley communities vary significantly based on what's included. Some cover golf membership, cable TV, and internet; others cover only security and common area maintenance with golf fees billed separately. Always ask for the full HOA fee disclosure, including sub-association fees, when evaluating a purchase.

        Fees listed represent general market ranges and are subject to change. Contact Shana for current HOA fee verification before making an offer.
