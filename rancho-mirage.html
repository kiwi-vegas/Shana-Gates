Privacy & Prestige

The Valley's Most
Exclusive Address

Rancho Mirage has long been the preferred address of presidents, captains of industry, and celebrities who prize privacy above all else. Frank Sinatra, Bob Hope, Gerald Ford, and the Annenbergs all called this city home — and their legacy of discretion and luxury lives on in every gated community and manicured estate.

Sunnylands, the legendary Annenberg estate and now a presidential retreat and public gardens, sits at the heart of Rancho Mirage. The Ritz-Carlton, Mission Hills Country Club, and Tamarisk Country Club anchor a social scene that balances exclusivity with a genuine sense of community.

For buyers seeking the pinnacle of Coachella Valley luxury — guard-gated streets, large lots, panoramic mountain views, and absolute privacy — Rancho Mirage delivers without compromise.

What Makes It Special

Living in
Rancho Mirage

🏛️

Sunnylands

The Annenberg estate has hosted every president since Eisenhower. Today it's a world-class retreat center and public gardens — a living monument to Rancho Mirage's elite heritage.

🔒

Ultimate Privacy

Many of Rancho Mirage's most desirable communities are fully guard-gated with 24-hour security. Few cities in California offer this level of peace of mind.

⛳

Mission Hills & Tamarisk

Two legendary country clubs sit within city limits. Mission Hills hosted the Dinah Shore Championship for decades; Tamarisk remains one of the valley's most coveted private clubs.

🏨

The Ritz-Carlton

The valley's finest luxury hotel anchors Rancho Mirage's hospitality scene — ideal for welcoming guests or simply indulging in a spa day steps from your estate.

⛰️

Mountain Panoramas

The Santa Rosa Mountains provide a dramatic backdrop visible from nearly every street. Sunsets here are world-class — fire and shadow across jagged peaks.

🌿

Quiet, Manicured Streets

Rancho Mirage's boulevards are wide, palm-lined, and immaculately maintained. The city invests heavily in its visual identity — and it shows at every turn.
