Vibrant & Authentic

Community, Arts &
Authentic Desert Living

Cathedral City occupies one of the most advantageous positions in the Coachella Valley — directly between Palm Springs and Rancho Mirage, offering easy access to both cities' best amenities while maintaining its own vibrant, authentic community identity.

Known for its welcoming LGBTQ+ community, colorful public murals, and an emerging arts scene, Cathedral City has developed a distinct personality that attracts buyers looking for a more genuine desert experience. The city's Cathedral City Cove neighborhood, with its hillside mid-century homes, is one of the valley's best-kept secrets for design enthusiasts on a budget.

With some of the valley's most accessible entry-level prices and a location that punches well above its weight, Cathedral City is increasingly attracting first-time buyers, investors, and buyers priced out of Palm Springs who want to remain close to all it offers.

What Makes It Special

Living in
Cathedral City

🏠

Cathedral City Cove

A hillside neighborhood of mid-century and contemporary homes with stunning mountain and valley views — Cathedral City's most architecturally interesting and rapidly appreciating area.

🎨

Vibrant Arts Scene

Public murals transform Cathedral City's walls into an outdoor gallery. The Camelot Theatres shows independent and international films; local studios showcase emerging desert artists.

🌈

Inclusive Community

Cathedral City has one of the valley's most established and celebrated LGBTQ+ communities, hosting an annual Pride festival and maintaining a deeply welcoming civic culture.

🎸

Cathedral City Jazz Festival

One of the desert's beloved annual events brings world-class jazz performers to Cathedral City's downtown — a signature piece of the city's cultural calendar.

💵

Best Value in the Valley

With median prices well below neighboring Palm Springs and Rancho Mirage, Cathedral City offers the same sunshine, same mountains, and nearby access to all valley amenities at a significant value advantage.

📍

Prime Central Location

Sitting between Palm Springs and Rancho Mirage means everything is within 10 minutes — Palm Springs International Airport, El Paseo shopping, the BNP Paribas Tennis Garden, and more.
