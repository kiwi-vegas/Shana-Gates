Why gated living in the Coachella Valley?

The Coachella Valley has a higher concentration of guard-gated communities than almost anywhere in the American West. That's not a coincidence — it's the product of 70 years of deliberate development around golf, privacy, and year-round resort living. When Frank Sinatra and Bob Hope chose the desert, they chose gated. When Fortune 500 executives and professional athletes choose their second home, they choose gated. Today, there are 80+ gated communities spanning every price point from $65K manufactured homes to $11M private estates.

What does a gate actually buy you? The obvious answer is security — but the real answer is community. Gated communities in the Coachella Valley are self-contained worlds: their own golf courses, pools, tennis programs, social calendars, and cultures. When you move inside the gates, you're not just buying a home; you're joining something.

The challenge is knowing which world fits you. That's what "Behind the Gates" is for — Shana's direct, honest, insider breakdown of every major gated community: what the HOA fee actually covers, what the vibe really is, and which buyers belong there.
